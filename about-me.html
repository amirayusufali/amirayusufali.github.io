ABOUT ME

Meet the Photographer

Huda Muhumed, a Somali-Finnish photographer who runs Huda Films. “I’ve always had a deep passion for capturing life’s most joyous moments, transforming fleeting experiences into memories that last a lifetime. What began as a personal hobby quickly turned into something more, as the growing demand from friends and family led me to officially pursue this passion as a career. The true reward for me lies in the reactions of those who see what I’ve captured. It’s the smiles, the tears, and the excitement that drive me to keep honing my craft. I pride myself on my keen eye for detail and my ability to create content that not only tells a story but also truly represents the essence of my clients. I make it a priority to listen to their desires and visions, ensuring that the final product reflects their unique personality and not just my personal preferences. My passion for creating content, both in videography and photography, is deeply rooted in my love for travel. Exploring different cultures and experiencing the world through various lenses has fueled my creativity and inspired me to continue bringing visions to life for people everywhere”.
