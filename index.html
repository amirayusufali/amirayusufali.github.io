ABOUT ME

Meet the Photographer

Huda Muhumed, a Somali-Finnish photographer who runs Huda Films. “I’ve always had a deep passion for capturing life’s most joyous moments, transforming fleeting experiences into memories that last a lifetime. What began as a personal hobby quickly turned into something more, as the growing demand from friends and family led me to officially pursue this passion as a career. The true reward for me lies in the reactions of those who see what I’ve captured. It’s the smiles, the tears, and the excitement that drive me to keep honing my craft.
My passion for creating content, both in videography and photography, is deeply rooted in my love for travel. Exploring different cultures and experiencing the world through various lenses has fueled my creativity and inspired me to continue bringing visions to life for people everywhere”.
